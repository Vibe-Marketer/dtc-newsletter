## Core 10 Techniques (Most Frequently Used)

### 1. Measure the Size
Add specific numbers to quantify the claim.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Lots of prompts included | 47 prompts included in the pack |
| Many products analyzed | 1,000+ products analyzed daily |
| Huge savings | Save $847 per month |

### 2. Measure the Speed
Add timeframes to show how fast results come.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Get descriptions quickly | Get your first product description in 30 seconds |
| Fast results | See results in 7 days or less |
| Quick setup | Launch your store in 48 hours |

### 3. Compare
Show contrast with alternatives: "Instead of X, you get Y"

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Save time on research | Instead of 3 hours researching, get answers in 3 minutes |
| Better than doing it yourself | What takes copywriters $500, this does for $9 |
| More efficient | 6x faster than manual research |

### 4. Metaphorize
Make abstract concepts concrete with vivid imagery.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| AI does the work | This prompt does the heavy lifting for you |
| Automates your process | Like having a copywriter on call 24/7 |
| Simplifies everything | Your AI assistant handles the grunt work |

### 5. Sensitize
Make the reader feel, smell, touch, see, or hear it.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Make your first sale | Imagine opening your phone to see your first sale notification |
| Earn passive income | Picture checking your bank account and seeing money you made while sleeping |
| Get freedom | Feel what it's like to work from a coffee shop on a Tuesday afternoon |

### 6. Demonstrate with Example
Show a prime example that proves the claim.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| People get results | Sarah used this exact prompt and got her first 3 sales in a week |
| It works | Last month, 47 subscribers landed their first sale using this method |
| Proven system | At 60mph, the loudest noise in this Rolls Royce is the electric clock |

### 7. State as Paradox
Something that seems contradictory but is true.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Easy to use | How a non-techie built a store in 48 hours |
| No experience needed | The method that works BECAUSE you're a beginner |
| Simple process | How doing LESS research finds BETTER products |

### 8. Remove Limitations
Show what the reader won't have to do or suffer.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Easy copywriting | Write product descriptions without any copywriting skills |
| No tech skills required | Build your store without touching a single line of code |
| Simple research | Find winning products without expensive tools |

### 9. Before and After
Contrast the two states explicitly.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Write descriptions faster | Before: 3 hours writing one product description. After: 3 minutes |
| Better product research | Before: Endless scrolling through AliExpress. After: AI finds winners for you |
| Save time | Before: 40 hours/week job. After: 10 hours/week running your store |

### 10. Challenge Reader
Turn it into a test or challenge.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| AI writes like a human | Can you tell which description was written by AI? |
| Professional quality | Quiz: Which product page made $10K last month? |
| Real results | Test yourself: Spot the winning product in 60 seconds |

## Additional High-Value Techniques

### 11. Connect Mechanism
Show HOW it works in the headline.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Finds winning products | AI analyzes 1,000 products to find your winner |
| Writes great copy | Pulls from 10,000 high-converting descriptions |
| Saves you time | Automates the 3 steps that eat 80% of your time |

### 12. Dramatize
Show the result in action with emotional impact.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Quit your job | My friends thought I was crazy to start an online store. Then came my first $500 day... |
| Success story | They laughed when I said I'd quit my job. Now they're asking for advice. |
| Get results | I was about to give up. Then this one change made everything click. |

### 13. Question Format
Turn the claim into an engaging question.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Make your first sale | Who else wants their first sale this week? |
| Easy product research | What if finding products took 15 minutes instead of 15 hours? |
| Passive income | Ready to make money while you sleep? |

### 14. Stress Newness
Emphasize that this is new/fresh.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| AI tool | Just discovered: The AI prompt that's changing product research |
| New method | Announcing: The method that's replacing 3-hour research sessions |
| Updated approach | NEW for 2026: The only AI workflow you need |

### 15. Stress Exclusivity
Make it feel rare or special.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Special tool | The tool nobody's talking about (yet) |
| Insider method | What 7-figure sellers use that beginners don't know |
| Limited access | Only available to newsletter subscribers |

### 16. Case History / Quote
Use a testimonial or case study.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| Works well | "I got my first sale 3 days after using this prompt" - New subscriber |
| Good results | "Wish I found this 6 months ago" - Mark, now full-time seller |
| Effective | "This replaced $500/month in tools" - Sarah, 4-month subscriber |

### 17. Condense / Interchange
Replace complex process with simple solution.

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| AI writing tool | A complete copywriter in a single prompt |
| Research software | Your entire product research team in one tab |
| Multiple tools | Everything you need, nothing you don't |

## Technique Selection Guide

**For vague claims:** Use Measure Size or Measure Speed
**For boring benefits:** Use Sensitize or Dramatize  
**For "me too" features:** Use Compare or Paradox
**For complex processes:** Use Condense or Remove Limitations
**For skeptical readers:** Use Demonstrate or Case History
**For action/urgency:** Use Before/After or Challenge

## DTCNews-Specific Examples

**Product Research Tool:**
- Generic: "Find winning products fast"
- Sized: "Analyze 1,000 products in 60 seconds"
- Compared: "What takes 3 hours manually, done in 3 minutes"
- Paradox: "Find winners by looking at FEWER products"

**AI Prompt Pack:**
- Generic: "Prompts for your store"
- Sized: "47 battle-tested prompts"
- Before/After: "Before: Staring at blank page. After: 10 product descriptions in 10 minutes"
- Limitation Removed: "Write like a pro without being a writer"

**Course or Training:**
- Generic: "Learn to build a store"
- Timed: "Launch in 48 hours, not 48 days"
- Example: "The exact steps Jake used to hit $5K/month"
- Exclusive: "The method 8-figure brands won't share"
