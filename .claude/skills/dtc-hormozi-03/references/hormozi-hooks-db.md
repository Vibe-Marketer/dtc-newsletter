## Subject Line Formulas

### Curiosity-Based
- "the [specific thing] that [unexpected result]"
- "why [common advice] is wrong"
- "the [adjective] way to [result] (nobody talks about this)"
- "I was wrong about [topic]"

### Direct Benefit
- "[Number]x [metric] from [simple change]"
- "The [specific number] [thing] that [result]"
- "[Timeframe] to your first [milestone]"

### Question-Based
- "what's actually stopping your [desired result]?"
- "still [common struggle]?"
- "would you [small investment] to [big result]?"

## Hook Components Checklist

Every hook needs:

- [ ] **CALL OUT** - Gets prospect to say "This is for me"
  - Identifies specific audience
  - Makes them feel seen
  - Creates instant recognition

- [ ] **CONDITION FOR VALUE** - Why they should consume
  - What they'll get from reading
  - The transformation/benefit
  - Curiosity gap or tension
