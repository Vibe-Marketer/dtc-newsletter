Here's what works: automated competitor tracking using SEMrush alerts + Facebook Ad Library notifications.

First, identify your top 5 direct competitors. Not aspirational brands. Companies selling the exact same products to your exact customer. Go to SEMrush and set up position tracking alerts for their domains. This pings you when they rank for new keywords or their ad spend changes dramatically.

Next, use Facebook's Ad Library to save each competitor's page. Then check "See ads from this Page" and bookmark those URLs. Set up a simple browser extension like Visualping to monitor these pages for changes. New ad creative? You know in 24 hours.

The real power move: create a Google Alert for "[competitor name] + discount" and "[competitor name] + sale." This catches their promotional calendar before it hits their email list.

Now you're not just copying. You're anticipating. When Competitor A tests a new product angle, you can launch your version while their creative is still in testing phase. When Competitor B runs their quarterly sale, you can counter-program with your own promotion.
