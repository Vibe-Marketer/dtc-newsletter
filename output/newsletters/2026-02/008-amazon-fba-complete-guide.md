When a competitor drops a new product, they've just done your expensive market research for free. They've validated demand. Proven the messaging. Identified the pain point. Now you swoop in with a better version before they can scale.

Here's the play that's working right now.

The moment you spot a competitor launch, grab their product page URL and run it through Archive.today. This creates a permanent snapshot of their exact messaging, pricing, and positioning. Do this within 24 hours before they start optimizing based on performance data.

Next, check their Facebook Ad Library. Screenshot every creative they're running. Note the hook, the pain point, the promise. They're spending money to test what resonates. You're getting their A/B test results for free.

Then hit Google Trends and type their exact product name. Look at the related queries section. This shows you what people are actually searching for when they discover this product type. Most brands miss these long-tail opportunities.
