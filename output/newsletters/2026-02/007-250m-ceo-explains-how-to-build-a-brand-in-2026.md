Here's exactly how to do it:

First, install the "Visualping" Chrome extension. It's free for up to 5 monitors. Go to your top 3 competitors' product pages and set up visual change monitoring. Set it to check every 6 hours. Now you'll get an email the second they launch something new, change pricing, or go out of stock.

Second, create a Google Alert for "[competitor name] + launch" and "[competitor name] + new product." This catches press releases, social announcements, and blog posts you'd otherwise miss.

Third, follow their key social accounts and turn on post notifications. Instagram and TikTok especially. Competitors soft-launch on social before updating their website.

Fourth, set up a simple Airtable with columns for: Competitor, Launch Date, Product Name, Price, Your Response Plan. When alerts fire, log everything. Patterns emerge fast.

The magic happens in the response speed. When Visualping alerts you that Competitor A launched a new skincare serum at $47, you can have your counter-product live on your site within 24 hours. Or adjust your pricing before they eat your market share.
